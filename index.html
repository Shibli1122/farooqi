Portfolio's:-

Electricity Generating tiles

I explored the concept of electricity-generating tiles, which convert mechanical energy from footsteps into electrical energy. Utilizing piezoelectric materials, these tiles produce electricity when pressure is applied. This innovative technology harnesses energy in high-footfall areas, such as train stations and shopping malls, providing a sustainable energy solution to power nearby devices like lights and sensors. My research into this technology highlights its potential for renewable energy generation and its applications in urban environments.

Image Classification System

Developed a Cat vs. Dog Image Classification System that utilizes a convolutional neural network (CNN) to distinguish between images of cats and dogs. The project involved gathering the Kaggle Cats and Dogs dataset, preprocessing images, and training the model using TensorFlow/Keras. Users can upload images to receive real-time classification, demonstrating the effectiveness of deep learning in image recognition with over 90% accuracy.

Inventory Tracking System

In my role, I implemented a real-time inventory tracking system that enables store managers to efficiently monitor stock levels. This system features automated updates synchronized with sales transactions, low stock alerts for items nearing predefined thresholds, and comprehensive sales trend reports that help identify popular products. As a result, the implementation significantly improved operational efficiency, minimized stockouts, and enhanced overall inventory management.
